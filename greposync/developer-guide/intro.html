Introduction

This guide is aimed at developers that want to contribute to the project. The intend is to make the internals easier to understand.

Domain-Driven Design

This project follows the Domain-Driven Design principles. This architecture is also commonly known as "Clean", "Onion" or "Hexagonal" architecture. There are sometimes subtle differences, but the general characteristics are as following:

  • Dependency Inversion

  • Ubiquitous Language

  • Rich domain models
